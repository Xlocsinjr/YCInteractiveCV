Who am I?

My name is Xander Locsin!

I am years old and I live in Amsterdam.

I have an interest in science and programming and I have been educated in Physics. Besides academics, I also have an artistic side with hobbies like 3d modeling, dancing and drawing.

In general, I am a bit of a reserved person but I have never had issues working in cooperation with others. I like understanding things, rather than just knowing. Since I like thinking about why things are, I consider myself an understanding person and I always try to be unbiased and considerate, and I value honesty and fairness.

Why programming?

Programming feels like puzzling to me. To solve a puzzle you consider what you know and how you can use that knowledge to logically arrive at a solution. In programming I would apply my knowledge on tools and languages to get to a working solution. Additionally I find it rewarding to see that the impact of your work is clearly visible because your efforts result in a functioning program or solution! This is why I enjoy programming!

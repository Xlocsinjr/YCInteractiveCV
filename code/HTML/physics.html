Physics

With an interest in the sciences and in the way the world works I chose to study Physics. Physics is the study of how the universe behaves on scales from galaxy clusters to the inside of atoms.

What I learned

Ofcourse in Physics I would learn about our world with courses like classical mechanics, special relativity, quantum mechanics, astronomy, thermal physics, and Electrodynamics, but also courses like math, practicals and research projects.

The behaviour of the universe can be described with math and so math is everywhere in the field of physics. In addition to knowledge of physics I have also developed mathematical skills in which critical thinking is important. To solve a problem in math, you consider what you know and how to apply that knowledge to get to a solution. In that regard, programming is not very different.
